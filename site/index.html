Questions

Answered straight

What is stone coated steel roofing?+

Steel panels protected by an aluminum zinc alloy and finished with kiln fired stone granules. Our Steel Series delivers the strength of metal with the look of tile, shake, or shingle — engineered to last 50 years or more.

What is the Storm Series?+

Our Storm Series is an SBS polymer modified shingle system built on Atlas StormMaster technology. The rubberized asphalt flexes instead of cracking, earns the highest impact rating available (Class 4), and is warranted for high wind coastal conditions.

Steel, Storm, or Fiberglass — which is right for my home?+

Steel Series is the 50 year, buy it once option with the strongest wind and fire performance. Storm Series adds Class 4 impact protection with a classic shingle look. Fiberglass Series is the proven architectural shingle at our friendliest price — lifetime limited warranty, 130 mph wind coverage, and algae protection. Our free assessment walks you through all three against your roof, budget, and neighborhood.

Are these systems good for hurricanes and coastal weather?+

Yes — that is the point. Both systems are rated for high wind coastal conditions, resist wind driven rain, and stand up to salt air far better than standard shingles. They are engineered for exactly what Virginia Beach and Hampton Roads weather delivers.

Will a Class 4 roof lower my insurance?+

Both systems carry Class 4 impact ratings — the highest available — and many insurers offer premium reductions for it. We provide the documentation for your carrier.

How much does a new roof cost in Virginia Beach?+

It depends on roof size, pitch, and the system you choose. We provide a free on site assessment and a fixed, itemized quote — usually the same week. Financing options are available.
